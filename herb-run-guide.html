Herb Run Tracker

📜 How to Use This Tracker
Your companion for efficient herb runs in RuneScape 3
1 Set Up Your Run
Choose your primary herb and yield per patch from the dropdowns above. Toggle any teleport unlocks you have to adjust your route times.
2 Check Your Loadout
Use the Pre-Run Loadout checklist to make sure you have your seeds, secateurs, juju potion, and teleports ready before starting.
3 Follow the Route
Click each patch card to check it off as you go. Cards show teleport methods, estimated travel times, and special notes. Drag cards to reorder your route.
4 Track Your Profits
Watch your stats update live as you complete patches. Use Update Prices to fetch live Grand Exchange prices. Check the Profit Breakdown and Run History for totals.
Pro Tips
Press ? to see all keyboard shortcuts. Use 1-8 to quickly toggle patches, Space to start/pause the timer, and C for compact mode during runs. If a patch dies, click the skull icon to mark it. Your progress is auto-saved to your browser.
